WELCOME TO LE MÉRIDIEN COIMBATORE

Luxury Business Hotel

Strategically positioned in one of India's most dynamic cities, Le Méridien Coimbatore is close to Coimbatore Airport (CJB), major businesses and a new IT park.

It's an ideal space for the sophisticated business professional. Inspiration can be found in our two restaurants serving authentic Indian cuisine. The lobby bar and lounge provides a modern communal venue, and the fitness centre, tennis court, pool and spa will invigorate body and mind.

With an emphasis on comfort and technology, our 254 guest rooms and suites evoke individuality and creativity. Featuring engaging décor and flexible work space, the rooms offer an urban escape for the well-travelled.
